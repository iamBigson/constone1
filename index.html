About The Cornerstone

We started with a simple question: what if help came with dignity, not paperwork?

The Cornerstone is a quiet response to that. No corporate structure, no red tape — just people offering a hand to people who have a plan. Inspired by faith and the idea that every person deserves a chance to build.

The 75% / 25% principle

We don't do interest. If you’re selected, you agree to return a quarter of what you received — only when you’re able. That quarter becomes help for someone else. It’s not profit; it’s a cycle of trust.

Who we are

Just a person who believes in second chances, hard work, and community. Real conversations, real help.
